Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Directorate of Employment & Craftsmen Training (DECT), Meghalaya. We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you register, login, and apply for various benefits on our platform.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information from you when you register and apply for services:

  • Personal Details like Name, Date of Birth, Gender
  • Address
  • Other relevant details necessary for the application process
How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Verify your identity and eligibility for benefits/services
  • Process your registration and services applications
  • Communicate with you about your applications and updates
Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may disclose your information to:

  • Government agencies or authorities as required by law
  • Service providers and contractors who assist us in providing our services
Security
We take the security of your information seriously and implement appropriate measures to safeguard it from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
