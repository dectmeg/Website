Guidelines for Registration with the Meghalaya Employment Exchanges

  1. All Indian citizens who have attained the age of 14 years and who normally have been residing in Meghalaya for a period of not less than 1 (one) year are eligible to register their name with the Employment Exchanges in the State.
  2. A candidate who wishes to register for Employment Assistance / Training / Services may visit www.dectmeg.nic.in for online registration or they may visit in person at the local Employment Exchange in the area where he/she normally resides.
  3. Once a candidate has registered online and completes the required fields, he/she will be issued with a Temporary Registration number which is automatically generated by the system.
  4. The candidate is then given 30 days' time within which he/she is required to visit the Local Employment Exchange for verification of his/her credentials/certificates & documents (in Original).
  5. Candidates whose documents and certificates are found in order will be issued with the Employment Exchange I.D. Card X-10.
  6. The registration is valid for a period of 3 years, and the candidate may renew his/her registration 2 months before the date of renewal or into 3 months after the expiry of registration online or in person.
  7. The following documents (in original) are required to be produced at the Local Employment Exchange for Completing the Registration Process:
    • School Certificate/Matric Certificate/Birth Certificate for proof of the Date of Birth.
    • Certificate(s) for proof of Qualification.
    • Caste/Tribe certificate in case of S.C./S.T./O.B.C. candidate.
    • In case of Ex-Servicemen, Certificate from Competent Authority.
    • In case of P.W.D. (Person With Disability) candidate - Disability certificate issued by Competent Authority.
    • In case of non-local Permanent Residents - A Permanent Resident Certificate (P.R.C.) issued by Competent Authority and E.P.I.C (if over 18 years) / Local Headman Certificate are required to be produced.
    • Temporary Residents are required to produce the Residence Certificate from the Local Headman and Dependent Certificate (if a minor)/E.P.I.C of the state where he originates (if over 18 years).
