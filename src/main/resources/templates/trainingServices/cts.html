Craftsmen Training Schemes

Implemented through the Government Industrial Training Institutes (G.I.T.Is) and the Private Industrial Training Institutes (P.I.T.Is). Craftsmen Training is imparted in 14 designated Engineering and 12 Non-Engineering Trades.

  • Objective is to prepare semi-skilled workers
  • Institutional Training through Government Industrial Training Institutes and Private Industrial Training Institutes.
  • Educational qualification varies from Class 8 pass to Class 12 pass depending upon the trades.
  • Duration of training varies from 6 months to 3 years.
  • Trainees after completion of training appear in the All India Trade Test (A.I.T.T.) held in the month of July every year.
  • Successful trainees are awarded the National Trade Certificate (N.T.C.) by the National Council for Vocational Training (N.C.T.V.).
  • National Trade Certificate (N.T.C.) is recognized for recruitment to subordinate posts within the country as well as abroad.

Admission Details:

Advertisement for admission is usually done in the month of May every year, and the new session starts in the month of August.
